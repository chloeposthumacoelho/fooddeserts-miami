microsoft digigirlz 2018 (microsoft sf)

Microsoft DigiGirlz Day (Event) was a one-day workshop dedicated to attaining more interest in technical jobs in the Computer Science field from young women.

Activities from this workshop included: building a circuit with lights and sewing it into a fabric brooch to make an electrically interactive fashion accessory (taught by Microsoft Creative Technologist, Kitty Yeung), programming aforementioned circuit using Arduino C to change the colors and speed of the lights, a workshop on building a personal brand, writing a resume, and how to gain internships and jobs specifically in the tech industry, and a one-on-one rotational Q&A session with women in multiple technical roles at Microsoft.
