What You Can Do

To work towards reducing the impact of the issues raised throughout this multimedia essay, we advise consumers to:

  • Buy products from brands that use sustainable palm oil where possible
  • Voice your concern to brands that do not use sustainable palm oil. A recommended way to do this is over a public forum such as social media. Companies are more likely to respond to complaints if they are public
  • Show your support using hashtags such as #DropDirtyPalmOil
  • Share the message to try and only support sustainable palm oil where possible
  • Share the multimedia sources or this multimedia essay to raise awareness
